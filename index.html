About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Artificial Intelligence/Data Science/Machine Learning at the School of Computer Science, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), supervised by Chair Prof. Guandong Xu and Dr. Qian Li. Previously, I received my Master of Information Technology in 2021 and Bachelor of Information Technology in 2018, both from UTS. I have a broad interest in Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Data Science, Recommender Systems, Causal Inference, and Machine Learning. My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of more transparent and effective data-driven systems across various domains. With more than three years industry working experiences and three years teaching experiences, I am proficient in programming languages such as SQL, Java, R, and Python, enabling me to develop effective solutions to complex problems.

My researach papers have been published in multiple top-tier conferences and journals in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, such as TKDE, WWW, SIGIR, CIKM, TOIS, TORS, Information Science, Neurocomputing, PAKDD, DASFAA, etc.
