About Me

I am currently a Research Associate at the City Future Research Center, University of New South Wales (UNSW), working under the supervision of Scientia Professor Chris Pettit. My current work focuses on advancing generative Artificial Intelligence (AI), particularly Large Language Model (LLM) techniques, for geospatial analytics to support urban planning and housing analytics in New South Wales (NSW).

I completed my PhD in AI and Data Science at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), supervised by Chair Professor Guandong Xu and Dr. Qian Li. I have authored 20+ publications in leading AI venues, such as TKDE, WWW, SIGIR, CIKM, TOIS, TORS, PAKDD, Neurocomputing, DASFAA, Information Science, et al. My expertise spans explainable AI, housing analytics, data science, causal inference, recommendation systems, and machine learning. With over three years of teaching and industry experience, and proficiency in programming languages such as SQL, Java, R, and Python, I have consistently delivered effective and practical solutions to complex challenges.
