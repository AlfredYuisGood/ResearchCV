Dianer Yu has been a Ph.D. candidate in the Data Science and Machine Intelligence (DSMI) Lab and an Academic Tutor at the School of Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney (UTS) since Jan 2022, under supervision of Prof. Guandong Xu and Dr. Qian Li. He received his MSc and BSc degree of IT from University of Technology Sydney. He has been awarded twice as Postgraduate Dean's List with outstanding outcomes. His general research interests lie primarily in data mining, causal inference and explainable machine learning. His papers have been published in the multiple top-tier conferences and journals in the field of data science.
